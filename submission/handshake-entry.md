## Project Title

Lokt: Text Your Workout Coach

## Submission Description

Lokt is an AI workout coach that turns text, voice, photo, and chat into editable workout drafts. Instead of bouncing between generators, trackers, and notes, users can ask for a workout, refine it like a conversation, and log it quickly once they start lifting. I built it to make training feel less manual, and used Codex to build the SwiftUI app, AI backend, parsing, review flows, and coach-chat UX.
